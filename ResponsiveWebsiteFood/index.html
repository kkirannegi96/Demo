About Me

Kiara Hearts is one part chef, one part filmmaker, and a generous dash of irreverent YouTube personality. Self-taught both behind and in front of the camera, her cooking show, Foodtake with Kiara, is enjoyed by millions of burgeoning chefs and foodies around the globe.

Her passion for teaching and experimenting in the kitchen is rivaled only by her love of film and television, both of which she endeavors to share from her INA, Payyanur kitchen.
